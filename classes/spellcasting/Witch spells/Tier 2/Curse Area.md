### 30 mana

### 1 hour casting time

### 5 mile range

### 30 hour duration

You channel your will into the surrounding area, and enchant it with a hex of your choice. you must continuously cast this spell for 30 days in a row in order to make its effects permanent. 

You may choose any number of specific creatures, groups of creatures, types of creatures etc. to be immune to the effects of the spell or specify that all but those creatures are immune to the spell. this list may be changed whenever you cast the spell on the same area with the same effect, even after it has been made permanent.

You may choose 1 of the following effects for the curse:

1) Protection (perception filter): 

  - the area is surrounded in a film which prevents weak minded creatures from percieving the area as interesting or unusual. a creature which would otherwise have found the area interesting or desire to explore it must unknowingly make an arcana check against your CORE DC. 
    - on a failure, the creature forgets any information which would have brought interest to the area, and is quickly distracted by something more interesting. On a success, the creature becomes immune to the affects of the curse for 24 hours.
    - if it again fails the check after this period, it again forgets all interesting information and is quickly distracted.
   
2) Protection (illusion):

   - the area is enchanted with an illusion which paints a scene of your choice. All creatures who see this area an fail an arcana check against your core DC will percieve an illusion which fits your desired image. each individual creature which fails the check may see slightly different things.
   - EXAMPLE: you choose for the area to be percieved as a calming prarie with a lake in the middle when in reality a camp of goblins has made its home inside. Any foliage will be adjusted to fit the scene, and living creatures will be adjusted to appear to be another creature which is much more fitting of the scene, such as a sheep in this example. Acts of violence by a creature disguised by the spell against someone who is not disguised will render that creature immune to the spell for 24 hours.
   - any creatures which detect the area must make an arcana check against your CORE DC or be fooled by the illusion. on a success, the creature will be aware that the area is enchanted with illusion, but will not be able to see beneath the illusion unless other measures are taken (such as an illusion breaking spell or magic item).
  
3) Protection (illness):

   - all creatures inside the area are immune to light disease and have resistance to poison damage. severe disease will have its effects reduced while inside the area (up to DM discretion), and the effects of tier 3 or higher magical disease will remain unaffected.
   - when a creature which aims to poison or inflict another with disease attempts to do so, they must make an arcana check against your CORE DC. on a failure, all creatures protected by the spell become alerted to the creature and their intent to poison.
   - Long rests and short rests in the area are always considered fully sheltered, even if sleeping outside or in poor weather.
  
4) Plight (antiheal):

   - whenever a creature in the area would heal hit points, they must first make an arcana check against your CORE DC. on a failure, they do not heal. on a success, they heal for half as much.
   - all creatures in the area are resistant to radiant damage and weak to necrotic.
   - all rests in the area have no positive effects for the mana or hp of the resting creature, although exhaustion is still lifted at half effectiveness.
   - Injuries may not be treated or cured in the area, and upon entering all creatures with treated injuries have their injuries un-treated.
   - while in the area of the spell, any creature which makes a check against poison or disease automatically fails.
