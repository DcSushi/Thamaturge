# THIS PAGE details what you should have on a finished character sheet for a level 1 character.

### CORE ATTRIBUTES

 - this is the most important piece of a level 1 character. it informs your skills desired skills, equipment, and class. whether you finalize your stats before or after deciding on your class and origin features is up to you.

    - the core attributes are as follows

Strength (STR or ST)

Agility (AGI or AG)

Fortitude (FOR or FO)

Mind (MIN or MI)

Willpower (WIL or WI)

Attunement (ATU or AT)

### CLASS

  - self explanatory why this is important

### ORIGIN FEATS/DEFECTS

  - these function as a way to add spice to the gameplay of your character outside of class features. they dont strictly affect flavor either, you can gain the benefits of the tail feature without actually having a tail.

### EQUIPMENT

  - this must be done after you know your attributes and class, since both influence the variety of weaponry and equipment you would prefer and be able to wield
    
     - also be sure to discuss with your GM which consumable items you might have, and how much cash your character starts with!

### SKILLS

  - these are informed by your core attributes, and determine what your character will have proficiency with, being referenced during skill checks (athletics determines how well you can push a boulder/climb a wall) and saving throws (focus will determine how likely you are to hold your focus when struck)

### HP, Mana, Speed, Dodge Value (DV), Armor (AR), Action points, etc.

  - these trackers are essential for all combats! dont go into one without them.
