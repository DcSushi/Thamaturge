### Warrior Heart: 

- Heart of Focus 
   + with one action point you may give your next attack +3 accuracy 

- Heart of Pride 
   + when you exceed a creatures DV when attacking them, you may reroll dice from the bonus damage if your weapon uses strength, or if your weapon uses agility you may apply one of the following effects:
     - heavy bleed: instead of dealing the bonus damage now, when the affected creature starts it's next 3 turns, it takes damage equal to half of that value.
     - hobble: for every 3 bonus dmg, you may reduce their movespeed by 1 tile on their next turn.
       
- Heart of Perseverance 
   + once per short rest when you reach 0 hp, you may begin to regenerate 1d6+2 now and at the start of every turn for the next 3 turns 
       + This gives a penalty of -2 on every d20 roll for the rest of the day 
 
- Stalwart heart 
   + at the end of your turn you gain an additional reaction point. 
   + this additional reaction point cannot be carried over to the next turn and is used first when you use any reaction
